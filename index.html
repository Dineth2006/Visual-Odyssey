Visual Odyssey

Graphic Designing Extravaganza!

Welcome to Our School's Visual Odyssey!

Attention all digital dream weavers and visual virtuosos! Prepare to dive headfirst into a world where pixels dance and creativity knows no bounds. Welcome to our eagerly anticipated Graphic Design Contest, where innovation meets imagination in the most spectacular fashion.

In this exhilarating event, we're calling upon all students with an eye for design to harness their artistic prowess and breathe life into our given topics. From the depths of fantasy to the realms of social commentary, this contest is your canvas, and your imagination is the only limit.

But what exactly are we searching for? We're on the lookout for graphic masterpieces that captivate, provoke thought, and ignite inspiration. Whether you're manipulating photographs, crafting digital illustrations, or designing captivating posters, this is your chance to leave a lasting impression with your creative genius.

This isn't just about showcasing technical skills. It's about storytelling, emotion, and pushing the boundaries of visual expression. So, fire up your design software, sharpen your digital brushes, and let your creativity run!

Rules & Regulations,

  • All the participants must be registered.
  • You must design a logo for our MRCM ICT society.
  • You have to do your manipulations using Adobe Photoshop. (you must send us the original project files as well)
  • You can use your own brushes or custom created ones.
  • Your creation must be unique (no copyright overlays)

Rules & Regulations,

  • You are not allowed to use any type of templates or AI help.
  • 1-4 member teams are allowed.
  • You can use 3rd party assets.(if you use you have to mention the links for those assets)
  • Accepted ways to submit
    • USB drives (for inter school participants only)
    • Sending links to Google drive via club website or Google Form.
